Entitlement to Stacks depends on your Terraform offering and license:

- **Terraform Cloud:** All organizations are entitled to Stacks, but the feature must be enabled at the organization level. If these fields are not returned, Stacks may not be enabled for your organization.
- **Terraform Enterprise:** Stacks are only available if your instance is licensed for them, through a RUM-based pricing model. In addition, Stacks must be enabled by a platform administrator at the instance level and for the organization.

If you are unsure whether your organization is entitled to Stacks, contact your **organization administrator**, **Terraform Enterprise administrator**, or **HashiCorp account representative** to confirm your license and feature availability.
