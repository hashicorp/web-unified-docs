# Setup ticketing - ServiceNow

Automatic ticketing of detected issues gives security and developer teams
visibility into potential issues so that they can plan, track, and report work.
Vault Radar supports ServiceNow as a ticketing tool.

## Prerequisites

- ServiceNow username and password

## Create a ServiceNow connection

1. Select **Settings** from the side navigation. Under the **Integration** menu, click **ServiceNow**.
   ![Nav start](/img/docs/vault-radar/integrations/servicenow/servicenow-int-1.png)

1. Click the **+ Connection** button.
   ![Connection start](/img/docs/vault-radar/integrations/servicenow/servicenow-int-2.png)

1. Enter a unique name for the connection.

1. Enter the username of a ServiceNow user with access to the desired account.

1. Enter the password for the username in step 4.

1. Enter the base URL of the account.

1. Click **Test & Save**.
   ![Connection success](/img/docs/vault-radar/integrations/servicenow/servicenow-int-3.png)

## Create a ServiceNow subscription

1. Click the **+ Subscription** button to begin the creation process.
   ![Subscription start](/img/docs/vault-radar/integrations/servicenow/servicenow-int-4.png)

1. Choose a name for the subscription and the saved filters you would like the subscription to use.

1. Choose the connection for the subscription output.

1. (Optional) Enter any [custom field](/hcp/docs/vault-radar/get-started/set-up-ticketing/servicenow/custom-fields) you wish to supply.

1. Click **Test & Save**.
   ![Subscription complete](/img/docs/vault-radar/integrations/servicenow/servicenow-int-5.png)

Vault Radar events matching your filters will create tickets in the specified ServiceNow project.
