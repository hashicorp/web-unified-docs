# Connect Infragraph to data sources

Before you can view and query data, you must connect your data sources to Infragraph and run a sync operation to populate the inventory. By default, Infragraph schedules connections to perform a sync operation every 24 hours. Refer to [Pause scheduled syncs](#pause-scheduled-syncs) and [Manually sync a connection](#manually-sync-a-connection) if you prefer to only sync a connection manually.


## Overview

Infragraph supports the following connections:

External data sources:

- [AWS](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/aws)
- [Azure](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/azure)
- [GitHub](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/github) 

Internal data sources:

- [HCP Terraform](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/hcp-terraform)
- [Terraform Enterprise](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/terraform-enterprise)
- [HCP Packer](/hcp/docs/infragraph/connections/packer)

HCP organizations are limited to one connection per connection type.

## Pause scheduled syncs

To pause a connection's scheduled sync operations, complete the following steps: 
1.	Click **Connections** in the left navigation panel. 
2.	Click the name of the connection that you want to update.
3.	Click the **Actions** dropdown.
4.	Click **Pause scheduled syncs**

To resume scheduled syncs, complete the following steps:

5.	Click **Connections** in the left navigation panel. 
6.	Click the name of the connection that you want to update.
7.	Click the **Actions** dropdown.
8.	Click **Resume scheduled syncs**


## Manually sync a connection

To manually sync a connection, complete the following steps:

1. Click **Connections** in the left navigation panel.
1. Click the ellipsis (**...**) next to the connection you want to manually sync.
1. Click **Sync connection**.

If you manually sync a connection you configured to sync automatically, Infragraph resets the sync cadence of that connection to run again in 24 hours.

Refer to the specific connection documentation for more information on sync behavior.

## Delete a connection

To delete a connection, complete the following steps:

1. Click **Connections** in the left navigation panel.
1. Click the ellipsis (**...**) next to the connection you want to delete
1. Click **Delete connection**.
