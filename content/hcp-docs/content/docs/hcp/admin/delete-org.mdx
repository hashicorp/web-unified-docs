# Delete an organization

Organization owners can delete their HCP organization directly in the [HCP Portal](https://portal.cloud.hashicorp.com/). Before you can delete an organization, you must resolve all outstanding items in the deletion checklist. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Prerequisites

You must have the `owner` role in the organization.

## Review the deletion checklist

Review the deletion checklist to verify that your organization is ready to be deleted. You must complete the requirements specified in the deletion checklist to proceed. To access the deletion checklist:

1. Log into HCP and open the Organization dashboard. 
1. Click **Manage organization**, then select **Organization settings**.
1. Click **Manage**, then select **Deletion checklist**.

The checklist groups requirements into four categories:

- **Billing**: Confirms there are no active contracts, unpaid invoices, or billable resources that would block deletion. 
- **IAM**: Confirms that users, groups, service principals, SSO, and SCIM are removed or disabled. Refer to the following topics for more information:
    - [Users](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/users)
    - [Groups](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/groups)
    - [SSO](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/sso)
    - [SCIM provisioning](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/users)
    -  [Service principles](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/service-principles)        
- **Infragraph**: Confirms there are no active Infragraph connections. Refer to the [Infragraph documentation](/hcp/docs/infragraph/) for more information. 
- **Resources**: Confirms that no project resources remain and that the organization has only one project. Refer to [Projects](/hcp/docs/hcp/admin/projects) for more information.

For any **To Do** items, click the ellipsis (**...**) and select the provided action to resolve the requirement.

Each requirement displays a status of **Done** or **To Do**. When all requirements are met, the page displays an **Eligible for deletion: Yes** banner and the **Delete organization** button becomes active.


## Delete your organization

After all checklist items show **Done** and the organization is eligible for deletion:

1. On the **Deletion checklist** page, click **Delete organization**.
1. In the **Delete organization?** confirmation modal, review the warning that this action cannot be undone.
1. In the **Confirm delete** field, type `DELETE` using all capital letters.
1. Click **Delete**.

After successful deletion, HCP redirects you to the organization list at `portal.cloud.hashicorp.com/orgs`. All organization owners receive an email confirming the deletion.
