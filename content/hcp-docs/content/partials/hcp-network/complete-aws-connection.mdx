1. Sign in to [the HCP Portal](https://portal.cloud.hashicorp.com/) and select your organization.
1. From the sidebar, click **HashiCorp Virtual Network**.
1. Click on an HVN in the **ID** column.
1. From the sidebar, click **Peering connections**.
1. Click the name of the connection in the **ID** column.
1. Copy the commands for inbound and outbound rules and run each command in your terminal.
