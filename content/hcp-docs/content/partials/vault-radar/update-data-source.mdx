1. Navigate to **Settings**.

1. Click **Data sources**.

1. Click the vertical ellipsis to the right of the data source.

1. To update the monitored data sources, click **Edit data sources**.

1. To update the authentication method, select the new authentication method from the **Select authentication method** dropdown.

1. To update the token, click **Edit data source host details**.

1. To delete the data source, click **Delete data source**.
