<Note>

If [Single Sign-On](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/sso) is enabled, manage the users
through the configured identity providers instead.

</Note>

1. Log into [HCP Portal](https://portal.cloud.hashicorp.com/) and choose your
   organization.
1. Click **Access Control (IAM)** in the sidebar and click **+Invite user**.
1. Enter their email address and click **Add**. You can repeat this step to
   continue adding users.
1. Choose a role from the **Assign role** drop-down menu and click **Invite**.
   Refer to the [User Permissions](#user-permissions) for information about the
   roles you can assign.

### Resend a pending invite

To resend an invite to a specific user:

1. Click **Access Control (IAM)** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Pending invites**.
3. Click on the dropdown of the user you want to resend an invite to and click **Resend invite**.
