### Snowflake DB password authentication ((#snowflake-db-password-auth))

| Announced | Expected end of support | Expected removal |
| :-------: | :---------------------: | :--------------: |
| APR 2025  | NOV 2025                | NOV 2025			|

Snowflake plans to disable password authentication for all users in
[November of 2025](https://www.snowflake.com/en/blog/blocking-single-factor-password-authentification).

As of Vault 1.20.x, the Snowflake database secrets engine supports key-pairs in
place of passwords for root credentials.
